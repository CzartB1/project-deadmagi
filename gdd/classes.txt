-- Roles --
Assassin - Single Unit DPS
Ravager - AoE DPS
Depleter - DoT DPS
Tank - Damage Sponge
Buffer - Gives Buffs + Morale Heal
Debuffer - Gives Debuffs + Morale Damage
Healer - Heals
Controller - Bind + Stun
Wildcard - Misc
-- Classes --
[Stahlmann] - Tank, Assassin
high hp, high dmg. Exosuit-wearing soldier with high caliber rifle.
- Suppressing Fire: Add 1 suppression to target and deal morale damage
- Slug: high damage. stun on crit

[Scholar] - Healer, Debuffer
Heals allies and debuffs individual enemies
- Reveal Weaknesses: apply weakness debuff
- Tonics: apply 3 regens

[Paladin] - Tank, Buffer
Inspires and protects allies
- Inspire: increase morale in an ally
- Protect: if a targeted unit takes damage (hopefully they target an ally), this guy takes it instead

[Berserker] - Tank, Ravager
Taunts and tanks. very simple.
- Reckless Attack: high damage. exhaustion to self on partial success or lower
- Rage: apply 3 rage to self. for every 5 rage that's in the unit while this action is executed, +1 exhaustion

[Jago] - Controller, Depleter
A martial artist. Bleed then bind. Can twist the blade to multiply bleed.
- Open wound: target receives damage and gains 3 bleed
- Twist the Blade: target receives damage and multiplies their bleed by 2

[Hemomancer] - Depleter, Assassin
Bleeds enemies. Have an attack that basically consumes enemy bleed, multiply it, then turn it into one time damage.
Basically explodes bleeding.
- Open wound: target receives damage and gains 3 bleed
- Heartburst: deal damage to the target equal to their bleed * 3

[Sicario] - Assassin, Ravager
Burst fire DPS. Guy with SMG.
- Suppressing Fire: Add 1 suppression to target and deal morale damage
- Spray and Pray: 3 attack burst

[Hellraiser] - Ravager, Depleter
Dragon's breath shotgun shell. Need I say more?

[Grafter] - Depleter, Unorthodox
Lifesteal or damage increase. Snowballer. Starts out squishy. Copy actions from a killed enemy until next enemy is copied..

[Draghul] - Debuffer, Depleter
Bleed and make them scared. Have an attack which explodes terror by how much bleed a unit have.

[Hexenjager] - Assassin, Debuffer
Snipes and debuff enemy abilities. Can focus to give themself more accuracy.

[Bladedancer] - Ravager, Buffer
Wide sword sweeps and buffs friendly agility and accuracy. Ripostes enemy attacks.

[Druid] - Depleter, healer
Poisons enemies and heal allies through fruits. 

[Fencer] - Debuffer, Assassin
Taunts and feints to force enemies to miss and strong dps. Ripostes enemy attacks.

[Kenshi] - Assassin, Healer
Glass Cannon that protects others to take their place when they're damaged, and can stun themself to heal

[Cloisterer] - Healer, Ravager
A squishy cleric that mostly supports and heals.
Have a nuke ability which stuns her for a long time. A really weak support with a free get outta jail card.

[Battle Angel] - Buffer, Ravager
DoT to multiple enemies while buffing allies. More supportive and make enemies squishier.

[Lucha] - Controller, Buffer
Binds enemies and gives morale to allies. Suplexes as bind breaker or submissions as dominance increase.

[Pyromancer] - Ravager, Depleter
AoE burn and bind breaker by heating metal.

[Astrologer] - Assassin, Debuffer
Stacks a special debuff to increase damage and nuke enemies.

[Jezailer] - Assassin, Controller
Kits to damage the enemies in bind and a strong finishing shot that stuns themself.

[Technobarbarian] - Tank, Depleter
A tanky guy with large bleed and terror kits. Pure agression more than Draghul's terror play.
